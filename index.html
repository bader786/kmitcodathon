About TechNova Hackathon

TechNova Hackathon 2025 is the premier coding event hosted by Global Innovate Labs, Hyderabad. Join us for an intense 24-hour coding marathon where innovators and coders collaborate to tackle real-world challenges. Showcase your skills, work in diverse teams, and compete for incredible prizes!

Event Details

  • Date: September 6, 2025, 9:00 PM - September 7, 2025, 9:00 PM
  • Venue: GIL Campus, Narayanguda, Hyderabad
  • Team Size: 5 members (at least 2 girls per team)
  • Categories: Web Development, AI/ML, Data Science, IoT, Cybersecurity
  • Prizes: ₹10,000 for the 1st prize-winning team, certificates for 5 runner-up teams
  • Registration Fee: ₹200 per member (₹1000 per team)
